Our Privacy Policy


When you visit our website, some information is collected through cookies to improve your use of our site and the services we offer. This information, excluding your identity information, is used to better provide the services on our site and improve the user experience. Cookies are used to remember your preferences, optimize our site's operation, and provide you with a better experience.

The collected information is not shared with third parties and is only used to increase our site's performance and improve our services. Users can prevent the use of cookies by changing their browser settings. However, please note that some parts of our site may not function properly in this case.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We take necessary technical and administrative measures to ensure the security of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, you can contact us.
