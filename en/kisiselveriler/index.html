Personal Data


Your personal data is collected only to meet legal requirements, fulfill your requests, and provide you with better service. The security of your personal data is our priority, and we take necessary technical and administrative measures within the framework of all relevant legislation, primarily the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK), to protect this data.

The collected personal data is only accessible by authorized personnel and is not shared with third parties without your consent. You have the right to learn for what purposes your personal data is processed, how it is stored, and with whom it is shared. Additionally, you can request access to your data, its correction, deletion, or anonymization.

If you have any questions or requests regarding your personal data, you can contact us through the communication channels specified in our Privacy Policy. The security and confidentiality of your data are of great importance to us.
